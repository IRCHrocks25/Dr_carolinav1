Dr. Carolina Castaños has spent more than 25 years in clinical practice and over 30 years studying the intersection of trauma, the nervous system, and human connection. What she observed again and again was this: even when meaningful psychological progress was taking place, some suffering remained deeply embedded in the body. People understood their experiences. They made real progress. Yet their nervous systems still reacted as if they were living in states of survival.

The ReConnect Method recognizes that lasting healing requires supporting all four dimensions of the whole person: your physiology — brain, nervous system, nutrition, movement; your psychology — emotions, thoughts, patterns; your relational world — how you connect with yourself and others; and your spiritual dimension — your sense of purpose, meaning, and connection to something larger. When even one pillar is neglected, suffering remains embedded.

Beyond one-on-one healing, Dr. Castaños brings the ReConnect Method to stages, organizations, and clinical training rooms around the world.

Why insight alone

You've been trying. You've been working on yourself. And still, here you are.

Here's what's actually happening: when your nervous system is stuck in survival mode, you lose access to the very self-awareness that could help you heal. You cannot see what you cannot see when your system is dysregulated.

What you need is not just conversation. You need a full-system reset.

Why ReConnect — A Note from Dr. Carolina Castaños

Where experience becomes wisdom
and knowledge becomes method.

I grew up moving between five countries, arriving as the new girl in places that were not always welcoming or kind. I learned early what it meant to be unseen, to not belong. At home, abandonment and neglect taught me my needs didn't matter.

Spirituality has always been central to my life. From early on, I felt called to help others — a deep sense that serving and healing was my purpose. I also felt a profound connection to the world, to nature, to something transcending the self. For me, spirituality isn't passive — it's active. It's about connection and action, understanding and doing. I'm driven to help, to serve, to improve things. My own early experiences of disconnection and my spiritual calling to serve led me naturally toward psychology and the work of healing.

Over more than 25 years of clinical work — in international organizations, recovery programs, schools, and private practice — and over 30 years as a student of the mind, training extensively with leading experts in neuroscience, somatic work, trauma therapy, and integrative healing, I repeatedly saw the same pattern. People gained insight. They understood their experiences. Yet their nervous systems continued to hold old patterns.

This taught me that real transformation requires addressing all four dimensions of the whole person: physiology, psychology, social connection, and spiritual meaning. That discovery, along with the wisdom I gained by healing my own wounds, became the ReConnect Method. This lived understanding allows me to help others heal their own pain — not just intellectually, but with deep recognition of what that suffering actually feels like. Because understanding suffering and knowing how to transform it are two different things. Together, they're extremely powerful.

My wounds taught me to recognize suffering. My training taught me to transform it. My purpose is to help you do both. — Dr. Carolina Castaños

The ReConnect Methodology

A full-system reset
across four dimensions.

At ReConnect, we don't treat symptoms. We transform systems. Healing happens across four fundamental dimensions — and lasting change requires attention to all of them. These four dimensions form the clinical foundation of the ReConnect Method and guide every program we offer.

— 01
Physiological
Regulate the Nervous System

Before therapy can work, the brain must be ready to receive it. We support brain function, nervous system regulation, and metabolic conditions that affect mental health through evidence-based neurotechnologies, breath work, movement, and targeted nutrition.

— 02
Psychological
Rewrite the Software

Understanding your patterns, emotions, and thoughts is essential. But insight alone doesn't create lasting change. This pillar addresses how trauma and stress shape your beliefs about yourself and the world.

Whether working individually, as a couple, or as a family, through psychotherapy, mindfulness practices, and self-compassion work, you learn to recognize your emotional patterns, develop compassion for yourself, and understand the power of emotions as messengers of your needs. You move from intellectual understanding to embodied transformation.

— 03
Relational
Breaking Cycles of Disconnection

Trauma lives in relationships. It must also heal in relationship. We work on:

  • Breaking generational patterns of conflict and disconnection
  • Building capacity for secure attachment in partnership
  • Transforming how you connect with yourself, your partner, and your children
"We heal in relationships — first with ourselves, and then with those we love." — Dr. Carolina Castaños
— 04
Spiritual
Reconnect with Meaning

Not religious. Sacred. This pillar reconnects you to:

  • A sense of meaning and purpose beyond symptom reduction
  • Compassionate presence with yourself and others
  • The truth of who you are when you are no longer defined by your pain

These four dimensions form the clinical foundation of the ReConnect Method. When they shift together, real transformation happens — creating stable, embodied, and sustainable change.

— The Human Story

Dr. Carolina Castaños understands disconnection not only professionally, but personally.

Born in Argentina and raised across five countries, she learned early what it feels like to arrive as an outsider — navigating new languages, unfamiliar cultures, and the quiet loneliness of not fully belonging. Later, as a doctoral student and immigrant in the United States, she experienced how even well-intentioned systems can leave people feeling unseen, reduced to symptoms rather than understood as individuals.

That experience shaped her vision for something different. ReConnect was created as a boutique integrative center where care is thoughtful, individualized, and designed around the whole person — supporting not only relief in the present, but long-term resilience and brain health over time.

Dr. Castaños works fluently in both Spanish and English and serves teens, adults, and couples from both English-speaking and Hispanic/Latin communities in Austin, Texas.
